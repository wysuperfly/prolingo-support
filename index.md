## Frequently Asked Questions

### What languages are supported?
proLingo+ supports 28 languages for speech recognition and 20+ language pairs for translation.

### Do I need an internet connection?
With the Local Engine, speech recognition works fully offline. Translation also works on-device without internet. Azure Speech requires an internet connection.

### How do I switch between Local Engine and Azure Speech?
Go to Settings > ASR Engine and select your preferred engine.

### Is my data private?
Yes. When using the Local Engine, all processing happens on your device. No audio is uploaded to any server. No account is required. No tracking. No ads.

### Is this a subscription?
No. proLingo+ is a one-time purchase. All features and future updates are included.
